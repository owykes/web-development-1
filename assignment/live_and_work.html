Live and Work

Lifestyle

As you can imagine life on Orkney can be removed from the many stresses of life, the folks here are community driven. Blending modern occupations with more traditonal ones you'd expect - fishing, farming and crafts. An equilibrium is acheived on the islands between the natural separation and the social traditions, festivals and storytelling past on from ancient visitors. There is a resilance and endurance of the people of Orkney that will never change, but also an adaptability that will always see the islands prosper socially and economically even further into the future.

Job Opportunities

Considering its remote nature, Orkney offers an impressive job market. With one of the highest employment rates in Scotland, Orkney offers a fantastic quality of life, blending flexibile, multiskilled postions with high job satisfaction. A world leader in renewable energy, strong agriculture and food production market, Tourism and heritage or many public sector roles. Come and see how Orkney can shape your future!
